Container Provisions

Standard Container Provisions

Material: Steel

Dimensions: 20ft or 40ft

Security: Locking mechanisms and seals

Ventilation: Vents on sides

Stackability: Designed for safe stacking

Accessibility: Double doors on one end

Weight Capacity: Typically up to 30 tons (depending on size)

Additional Provisions (Depending on Type)

Refrigerated Containers (Reefers): Temperature control for perishable goods

Open Top Containers: Suitable for tall cargo that needs top loading

Flat Rack Containers: Used for oversized cargo, with collapsible sides

Tank Containers: Designed for transporting liquids or gases, with specialized fittings

Specialized Containers: Customized for specific cargo needs (e.g., automobiles, hazardous materials)
